Root Cause
- The selfhost AST is stored as a flat statement array.
- `CanEmitAstStmtRange` and `EmitAstStmtRange` walked a range with `i := i + 1`.
- For nested statements, especially `if`/`else if`, this treated subtree members as if they were sibling root statements.
- That caused repeated re-walking of nested subtrees and led to an effective hang during stage1 self-compilation.

Fix Applied
- Updated [selfhost/compiler_codegen.inc](/home/kamitani/kpascal/selfhost/compiler_codegen.inc):
  - Added `AstStmtSpan(stmt_idx)` to compute subtree span for flat AST statements.
  - Changed `CanEmitAstStmtRange` to advance by subtree span instead of `+1`.
  - Changed `EmitAstStmtRange` to advance by subtree span instead of `+1`.
  - Changed `RootHasAstCallStmt` to advance by subtree span instead of `+1`.
- Did not keep the earlier experimental counting change in `selfhost/compiler_stmt.inc`; the final fix is on the codegen-side walker.
